A Mission We Stand For

In a world where millions go to bed hungry, Zero Hunger is more than a goal—it's a global call to action. Our team of passionate students has come together to raise awareness and inspire change through this platform.

At Sathkara.lk, we are a team of passionate students who believe that awareness is the first step toward change. This platform is our voice — built to inform, engage, and empower the people of Sri Lanka and beyond.
