Eco-Friendly Farming Solutions

  • Agroecology: Implementing farming methods that work with nature rather than against it
  • Precision Agriculture: Using technology to optimize water and fertilizer use
  • Permaculture: Designing sustainable and self-sufficient agricultural systems
  • Cover Cropping: Protecting soil health between planting seasons
  • Integrated Pest Management: Reducing pesticide use through natural pest control
  • Urban Farming: Bringing food production closer to consumers in Sri Lankan cities
  • Traditional Sri Lankan Methods: Reviving ancient techniques like Chena cultivation with modern sustainability
