Understanding Hunger in Sri Lanka

Facts About Hunger in Sri Lanka

Estate Sector Food Insecurity

In 2023, 51% of estate sector households faced food insecurity. This region remains critically vulnerable.

Read Source →

Nationwide Insecurity

Moderate food insecurity rose to 24% in 2023, up from 17% earlier that year. Progress is unstable.

Read Source →

Children in Crisis

56,000 children in Sri Lanka suffer from severe malnutrition. One-third of families lack food security.

Read Source →

Coping Strategies

42% of households are skipping meals or cutting portions to deal with food shortages.

Food Spending Pressure

Many households spend over 62% of their income on food alone, leaving little for other needs.
